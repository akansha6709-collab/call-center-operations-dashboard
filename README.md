# Call Center Operations Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview
This project analyzes call center operational performance to help CX and operations managers monitor service levels, identify SLA risks, and improve agent efficiency.  
The dashboard replicates reporting scenarios commonly used in BPO and customer support environments.

## Business Problem
Call center managers need real-time visibility into call volume, response times, and customer experience metrics to:
- Prevent SLA breaches
- Optimize staffing and agent utilization
- Improve customer satisfaction

## Key KPIs Tracked
- **Call Volume** – Total inbound calls handled over time
- **Average Speed of Answer (ASA)** – Measures customer wait time
- **Average Call Duration** – Identifies handling efficiency
- **Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)** – Tracks service quality
- **Call Sentiment** – Understands customer mood and interaction quality
- **Call Trends by Channel and Reason** – Identifies demand drivers

## Dashboard Highlights
- Trend analysis to identify peak call hours and workload patterns  
- SLA monitoring using ASA and call duration metrics  
- Channel-wise and reason-wise breakdown of call drivers  
- High-level performance view for daily and weekly operations reviews  

## Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX measures, and interactive dashboards  
- **Excel** – Data cleaning and preparation  

## Use Case
Designed for CX leaders, operations managers, and analysts to support:
- Daily performance tracking  
- Weekly operational reviews  
- Data-driven staffing and process improvement decisions  

## Notes
This is a self-initiated analytics project built to demonstrate practical Power BI and operational analytics skills relevant to BPO and customer support roles.
